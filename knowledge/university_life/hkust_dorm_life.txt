Dorm life at HKUST:
- Halls are allocated based on points system for year 1 students.
- UG Halls have double/triple rooms, some with sea view.
- Monthly hall fee around HKD 15,000–20,000 including utilities and meals.
- Common facilities: study rooms, gyms, common rooms, laundry.
- Rules: No overnight guests without permission, quiet hours after 11pm.
- Tips: Join hall activities early to make friends!
